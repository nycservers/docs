# Quick Start

Total time: **3–5 minutes**

<Callout type="info">
If you already ordered, skip to **[Connect To Your VPS](/connect/index)**.
</Callout>

---

## 1) Select Your VPS Plan

1. Go to **newyorkcityservers.com** and open the **VPS** plans.
2. Choose the region closest to your **broker’s matching engine** (e.g., LD4 for many UK/EU brokers, NY4 for US).
3. Click **Order**.

![Plan selection screenshot placeholder](../images/quickstart-plan-selection.png)

<Callout type="tip">
Not sure which location is best? Use the **[Latency Checker](https://newyorkcityservers.com/broker-latency)** to find the lowest ping to your broker.
</Callout>

---

## 2) Complete the Order

1. Review plan, **billing cycle**, and region.
2. Create or sign into your **Client Area** account.
3. Enter payment details and submit the order.

![Checkout screenshot placeholder](../images/quickstart-checkout.png)

<Callout type="warning">
Make sure your **email address is correct**—we’ll send your VPS details there.
</Callout>

---

## 3) Receive Your VPS Details

- Provisioning is automatic. You’ll receive an email with:
  - **Server IP / Hostname**
  - **Username/Password**
  - **Region / Datacenter**
- You can also view details anytime in the **[Client Area](https://newyorkcityservers.com/billing/)** under **Services → Your VPS**.

![Service details screenshot placeholder](../images/quickstart-service-details.png)
